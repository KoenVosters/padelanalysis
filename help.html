Padel Score Analyzer - Help

How to Use

The Padel Score Analyzer calculates ranking points based on your padel match performance. It also allows you to enter additional scores to get a view of the risk you are taking if you play another tournament or match.

Input Fields

Player Name

Enter the name of the player whose performance you want to analyze.

Tournament Types

The analyzer supports two types of tournaments:

  • Tournaments: Regular and Gemengd (Mixed) categories
  • Interclub: Regular and Gemengd (Mixed) categories

Match Results

For each tournament type and category, enter:

  • Pool Wins: Number of matches won during the pool phase against people of your own rank or a higher rank.
  • Pool Defeats: Number of matches lost during the pool phase against people of your own rank or a lower rank.
  • Finals Wins: Number of matches won in the finals against people of your own rank or a higher rank.
  • Finals Defeats: Number of matches lost in the finals against people of your own rank or a lower rank.

Scoring Formula

Points are calculated using the following formulas:

Win Points (WP): (Pool Wins × 1 + Finals Wins × 1.5) × Category Multiplier

Defeat Points (VP): (Pool Defeats × 1 + Finals Defeats × 0.5) × Category Multiplier

Category Multiplier: Regular = 1.0 | Mixed = 0.4 | Interclub = 0.8 | Interclub Mixed = 0.4

Deducted points: Points deducted based on the amount of matches you played (lose 1 defeat point per 12 matches)

Understanding Results

Results Display

After clicking "Calculate Points", you'll see a detailed breakdown including:

  • Win Points (WP): Points earned from victories
  • Defeat Points (VP): Points earned from defeats - played matches divided by 12
  • Total Points: Combined score for ranking purposes

Results are shown for each tournament type and category combination.

Tips

  • Enter 0 for any category where the player didn't participate
  • Finals wins are weighted more heavily (1.5x) than pool wins (1x)
  • Mixed/Gemengd tournaments are weighted at 0.4x the value of regular tournaments
  • Interclub tournaments are weighted at 0.8x the value of regular tournaments
  • Interclub Mixed tournaments are weighted at 0.4x the value of regular tournaments
  • Use the analyzer to track performance progression over multiple tournaments
